About ZestRoot Farm

Located in the heart of agricultural country, ZestRoot Farm has been dedicated to growing premium garlic for over a decade. Our commitment to sustainable farming practices and attention to detail ensures that every bulb we harvest meets the highest standards of quality and flavor.

We specialize in heirloom varieties that have been carefully selected for their robust flavor profiles and superior storage capabilities. Our garlic is grown without synthetic pesticides, using only organic methods that respect both the land and our customers' health.

Our Growing Process

1

Soil Preparation

We begin with rich, well-draining soil amended with organic compost and natural fertilizers.

2

Planting

Garlic cloves are planted in fall, allowing them to develop strong root systems over winter.

3

Care & Maintenance

Regular weeding, watering, and organic pest management throughout the growing season.

4
