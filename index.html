About Wildling Acres

Nestled in the fertile soils of Salem, Ohio, Wildling Acres is a family-owned organic garlic farm dedicated to producing the finest hardneck garlic. For over 10 years, we've used sustainable, chemical-free methods to cultivate heirloom varieties that thrive in our northern climate. Our garlic is known for its bold flavors, large cloves, and long shelf life—ideal for home cooks, gardeners, and gourmet enthusiasts.

We prioritize soil health with crop rotation, cover crops, and natural compost, ensuring our products are not only delicious but also environmentally responsible. Whether you're buying for your kitchen or to plant in your garden, our garlic ships fresh and ready nationwide.

Planting Guide for Home Gardeners

Buying our garlic for planting? Follow these tips for success in your own backyard:

  1. Timing: Plant in fall (September-November) for harvest next summer.
  2. Soil: Well-draining, fertile soil with pH 6.5-7.0.
  3. Spacing: 6 inches apart, 2-3 inches deep, pointy end up.
  4. Care: Mulch for weed control, water moderately, harvest when leaves brown.
  5. Tip: Our hardneck varieties are ideal for zones 3-6, like Ohio's climate.
